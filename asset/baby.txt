All societies define childhood within certain parameters. From infancy to adolescence, 
there are societal expectations throughout the various stages of children's development 
concerning their capabilities and limitations, as well as how they should act and look.
Clothing plays an integral role of the "look" of childhood in every era. An overview
history of children's clothing provides insights into changes in child-rearing theory 
and practice, gender roles, the position of children in society, 
and similarities and differences between children's and adults' clothing.
